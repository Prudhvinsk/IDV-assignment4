Singapore Population by Subzone (June 2024)

Choropleth

This interactive choropleth map visualizes the population distribution across Singapore’s subzones in June 2024, allowing users to explore spatial patterns and variations.

Datasets

  • Singapore Population Geospatial Timeseries – Singstat: View source
  • Master Plan 2019 Subzone Boundaries – Data.gov.sg: View source

Visualisation

Use the filters for Gender, Age Group, and Dwelling Type to subset the population data. Hover over any subzone polygon to see its name, planning area, and population count.
